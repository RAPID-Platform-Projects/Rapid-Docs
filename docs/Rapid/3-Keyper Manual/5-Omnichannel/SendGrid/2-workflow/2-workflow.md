# SendGrid and Workflow


## Introduction

In this chapter we will look at how we can use your SendGrid integration (created in the previous chapter) inside Workflow to automate the sending of emails.

### Initail Setup

To get started, first navigate to and create a new Workflow process.

Navigate to workflow:

![alt text](<navigate to workflow.png>)

Next, either open and exsisting, or create a new Workflow process:

![alt text](<create new process.png>)

Click 'New Process Diagram', then fill in the relevent information. At minimum you will require a title.
Finally, click create and you will see your new Diagram at the top of the list.

Click the title of your new Diagram to open:

![alt text](<open diagram.png>)
